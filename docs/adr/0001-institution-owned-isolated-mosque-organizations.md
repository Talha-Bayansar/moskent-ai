# Institution-owned and isolated Mosque Organizations

Each Mosque Organization is a durable institutional boundary rather than an asset owned by its founder: at least two verified Organization Administrators share governance, critical changes require two-person approval, and leadership changes use a verified handover. A User Account may enter multiple Mosque Organizations, but Person records, permissions, operational data, Copilot context, and administration remain isolated because convenience across organizations must not weaken governance, privacy, or auditability.
